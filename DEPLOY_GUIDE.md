# How to Host Your Birthday Website for Free 🌐

Here are two very easy ways to put your website online so you can share the link with Heer!

## Option 1: Netlify Drop (Easiest & Fastest) 🚀

1.  Go to this website: [https://app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
2.  You will see a box that says **"Drag and drop your site folder here"**.
3.  Open your computer's file explorer to where your `heer` folder is.
4.  **Drag and drop the entire `heer` folder** into that box on the website.
5.  Wait a few seconds... and boom! You will get a link (e.g., `random-name-12345.netlify.app`).
6.  Click the link to test it. You can share this link with Heer!

*Note: If you want to change the link name (like `heer-birthday.netlify.app`), you will need to sign up for a free Netlify account.*

---

## Option 2: GitHub Pages (Permanent) 🐱

1.  Go to [GitHub.com](https://github.com/) and sign in (or create an account).
2.  Click the **+** icon in the top right and select **New repository**.
3.  Name it something like `heer-birthday`.
4.  Check the box **"Add a README file"** and click **Create repository**.
5.  Click **Add file** > **Upload files**.
6.  Drag and drop ALL your files (`index.html`, `style.css`, `script.js`, and the `images` folder) into the box.
7.  Click **Commit changes**.
8.  Go to **Settings** > **Pages** (on the left menu).
9.  Under **Branch**, select `main` and click **Save**.
10. Wait a minute, refresh the page, and you will see your live link!
